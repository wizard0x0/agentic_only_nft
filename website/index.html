2 How it works
1

On-chain AA wallet check

When mint() is called, the contract checks msg.sender.code.length > 0. Only smart contract accounts (AA wallets) have bytecode deployed at their address.

2

OKX Agentic Wallet = AA contract

The OKX Agentic Wallet on X Layer is an Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) smart contract wallet. It passes the check. Regular MetaMask/hardware wallets are EOAs — they fail.

3

One NFT per agent, forever

Each AA wallet can mint exactly one AgenticNFT. The hasMinted mapping is permanent. The NFT is transferable after minting — but re-minting is impossible.

4

Free on X Layer

X Layer charges zero gas fees for transactions via OKX Agentic Wallet. Minting costs nothing. No ETH, no OKB required.
